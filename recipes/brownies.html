Brownies

Best Brownies Recipe

This is the last brownie recipe you'll ever need.

Ingredients

Brownies

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • cocoa powder
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder

Frosting

  • butter
  • cocoa powder
  • honey
  • vanilla extract
  • confectioners' sugar

Steps

Make the brownies

  1. Melt the butter on the stove
  2. then remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients
  3. Beat in the dry ingredients
  4. Spread the batter in the pan and bake

Make the frosting

  1. Stir the frosting ingredients together until well combined
  2. then frost the brownies while they are still warm
  3. Cut into squares when cool
