Waffles

Best Waffle Recipe

This is the last waffle recipe you'll ever need.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flour
  • milk
  • oil
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • vanilla
  • cooking spray

Steps

Make the batter

  1. Whisk the eggs
  2. then add the flour, milk, and oil
  3. Whisk in the sugar
  4. Stir in the remaining ingredients

Make the waffles

  1. Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron
  2. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter
